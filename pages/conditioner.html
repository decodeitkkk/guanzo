Key Ingredients
Keratin
As the primary structural protein in your hair, keratin plays a pivotal role in maintaining its strength and vitality.
Argan Oil
Rich in vitamins and minerals, argan oil nourishes and shields your hair from damage while imparting a lustrous, detangled finish.
Hydrolyzed Keratin
This specialized form of keratin features smaller molecules that penetrate deep into the hair, reinforcing its core.
Panthenol
A variant of vitamin B5, panthenol hydrates your hair and scalp, ensuring they remain moisturized.
Glycerin
Functioning as a humectant, glycerin attracts and retains moisture, enhancing your hair's overall health.
How To Use
