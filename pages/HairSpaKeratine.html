Hair Conditioner

Hair consists of 97% protein so it is essential to replenish and restore this major component with any product used on the hair. All chemical services such as coloring, bleaching or highlighting corrodes the hair causing a protein imbalance. As such, hair needs to be fed with keratin protein infusions to re-balance.A key ingredient to fulfill this need is hydrolyzed wheat protein. This is a water soluble protein derived from wheat. It’s able to penetrate the inner cortex of each strand and has the ability to strengthen and moisturize hair as well as increase its ability to absorb and maintain moisture.
