Guanzo Enzo Red Spray

Ratings:
Hair requires a natural sebum (oil) balance in order to be manageable and pliable. Hair lacking this balance is dry and unmanageable and is the root cause of most “bad hair days!” Enzo Holistic utilizes macadamia nut oil which has a synergistic relationship with capillary sebum thereby increasing the strength of the hair strand right from the root. It is thus capable of moisturizing and strengthening rough, dry, frizzy and limp hair. This holistic oil also puts a protective coating on hair follicles and the scalp thus preventing moisture loss. This is accomplished without making the hair or scalp greasy and is readily absorbed into the hair shaft ensuring maximum protection.
